Part 1
"the tributes"







chapter 1

The story starts of with the main character, Katniss everdeen , waking up, finding that her sister Prim (short for Primrose) whom she had been sharing the bed with, had gone to curl up with her mother on a make shift bed on the floor. After that she gets ready to go and hunt, finding the goat milk cheese, her sister had left her. In the woods she meets up with her best friend, Gale. They go along with their usual routine, hunting, gathering, and setting and checking traps. After that, they head home to get ready for the hunger games, a holiday were they basically watch people from each district kill each other, and this year's tribute is Katniss' sister, Primrose Everdeen.






Chapter 2

This news leaves Katniss in shock, trying to gather herself. Not knowing what else to do, she volunteers her. Her sister freaks out and Gale brings her to her mother. Continuing with the drawing of the tributes, the boy tribute is a boy named Peeta Mellark, who Katniss barely knows, even though his actions are what turned her into the BA hunter she is to day. Then surprisingly Peeta's dad visits her, he brings her cookies and says he'll make sure Prim does not starve. After him it's the Mayor's daughter, Madge, she gives Katniss her pin and asks that she wear it. After her Gale comes in, all he does is hold her. After their all done they get on a train to the capital.






Chapter 4

He punches Haymitch back and Katniss stabs a knife in the table, then throws it in the wall. Haymitch is impressed with her knife skills and Peeta's fighting.He decides that they actually have a chance to survive in the arena.

Getting off the train, the tributes have to meet with their stylists, who are supposed to make them look as appealing as possible. Which includes a lot of waxing on their part. After the prep team is done with her, Katniss meats her stylist and discuses her outfit. Her stylist, Cinna, has a creative eye that could never be topped. The crowd roars with approval.






Chapter 6

After the ceremonies are threw, they are taken to the training area, where they'll be living for the remainder of the competition. After an amazing meal, Peeta takes Katniss to the roof. They talk, and I realized half way threw this part that Peeta likes Katniss, and is trying to see if she's dating Gale. After that's over, and Katniss is done trying to figure out Peeta's angel, they each go back to their rooms.






Chapter 7

The next day they are taken to the area where their going to be trained not to die in the arena. There are stations where you can learn to trap, make knots, and other thing that may help them survive. The last day is when the Gamemakers evaluate their skills. Katniss is last to go, and it doesn't go as well as expected. The Gamemakers are tiered of watching all the contestants, and most of them are drunk. Katniss get angry when they don't pay attention to her shooting arrows. So she takes aim and shoots the apple out of the incoming roasted pigs mouth. Then she storms out.






Chapter 8

Completely irritated by the Gamemakers, Katniss storms out of the training area and up to her room. There she stays the whole night. The next day the Gamemakers announce their scores. Peeta Gets an 8, which is well enough. And to everyone's surprise, Katniss gets the highest score, an 11. The Gamemakers must have been impressed. Then they congratulate each other, and start to talk about the interviews. Peeta decides he wants to be coached separately.






Chapter 9

The next day is the interviews, Katniss spends 4 hours with Effie going over walking and waving. All that just leaves Katniss annoyed. The next 4 hours with Haymish isn't much better seance he's drunk, and doesnt help Katniss at all. Then comes the actual interviews. Katniss is scared out of her mind, even in the stunning dress Cinna made her. Cinna seams to be the only one who helped Katniss, even with the interviews. When it comes to her turn she is just honest. She has the crowd eating out of her hands by the time shes done. Then it Peeta's turn, he's humble and likable. But in the end the thing that really clutches the crowd is his confession of love for Katniss.

Chapter 19

After she beets her self up for yelling his name, she sets out to find Peeta. She finds him by a stream were he had perfectly camofloged himself to blend in to the rocks next to the water. Most of this chapter is taken up by Katniss telling Peeta the story of how she got Prims goat. Then theres an announcement that all the tributes need something, and if they all go to the cornicopia at midnight then they'll recieve that item. Peeta is still in no condition to walk, and doesn't want Katniss to go cause he thinks its dangerouse. Haymitch sends Katniss sleeping medicen so she can put Peeta to sleep. And Katniss tricks him in to taking it.






Chapter 21

Katniss heads to the Cornicopia. When she's there the first girl is runs to grab her pack and gets away quickly. Katniss runs up next and gets a knife thrown at her. Then she's almost killed and would have been if not for Thresh, the other tribute from district 11. He kills the girl that threw the knife and if it wern't for Katniss teaming up with Rue Thresh would have killed her. she reaches the cave and plunges the needle from the medicen in Peeta's arm.






Chapter 22

Eventually, Katniss and Peeta must face off with Cato, the only other surviving tribute, but before that they are all pursued by wild dogs which are actually genetically mutated killing machines. Finally, Katniss shoots Cato and he falls into the pack. They've won, right? Wrong. An announcer comes back on and says the rules have changed back: only one winner allowed.






Chapter 23

Katniss and Peeta can't kill each other, so they make a show of taking poisonous berries in an act of double suicide. Fortunately, the announcer comes back on before they can kill themselves, and says that they win. They defeated the Hunger Games! Or wait, did they?






Chapter 24

Katniss and Peeta keep up the star-crossed lovers routine for the post-games reunion and interview, knowing that this is the only way to keep from being punished by the Capitol for the rebellious trick with the poisonous berries. Eventually Katniss figures out that Peeta really is in love with her – he wasn't acting at all – and he figures out that she wasn't ever in love with him. Oops. As the train pulls into District 12, they put on a happy face for the camera, take each other's hands and step onto the platform.
